About Us

Nina Hotels is a popular hospitality and relaxation venue located at Plot 3 Romanus Nzekwe Street, Phase 4, Kubwa, Abuja. Known for its extensive sports and recreational facilities, it functions as both a low-key hotel and an active social center for families, groups, and business travelers.
 
Amenities and Recreation
 According to https://www.tripadvisor.com/Hotel_Review-g293825-d19493131-Reviews-Nina_Hotels-Abuja_Federal_Capital_Territory.html ⁠guest reviews on TripAdvisor, the property stands out for its extensive sports center.
 
Recreation:

It features outdoor courts for basketball, lawn tennis, table tennis, badminton, volleyball, and a small football pitch. An open gym and an outdoor swimming pool are also on-site.
Dining & Nightlife:

The hotel has an on-site restaurant serving local and international dishes, alongside an open-air bar. It is also known to host vibrant weekend events, including an open-air night club layout..
 
Standard Services:

Guests receive free Wi-Fi, complimentary breakfast, free parking, and room service. Rooms are equipped with air conditioning and flat-screen TVs.
 

Guest Reception:

The hotel maintains a 4/5 star ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ rating from over 500 user reviews on Google.

Pros: Visitors frequently praise the tastiness of the meals, fair pricing structures (with discounts available for long-term stays), and robust security.
Cons: Some travelers note that while the physical structures and rooms are highly comfortable, customer service can occasionally feel slow or short-staffed during peak periods.
Location Note: While the hotel provides an excellent escape inside its walls, its interior location in Kubwa means the surrounding access roads can occasionally be bumpy, though completely motorable.
